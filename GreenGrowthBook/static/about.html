menu_book About the Book

Green Growth That Works is the first practical guide to bring together pragmatic finance and policy tools that can make investment in natural capital both attractive and commonplace. Here, you can explore the real-world case studies featured in the book. These examples show how governments, businesses, NGOs and other groups are channeling economic resources towards conservation and restoration, for the benefit of people and nature. Click the question mark button on the right get a quick tutorial on navigating this site.

To learn more about the featured case studies, you can purchase Green Growth That Works from Island Press (and use discount code GROWTH for 20% off), Amazon, or your favorite bookstore.

live_help How to use the Green Growth Explorer?

This application gives a brief overview of the cases studied in Green Growth That Works.

Explore cases either by using the left and right arrows, or by selecting them in the left-hand menu.

The right-hand side panel displays content specific to the case being viewed. It offers a brief summary of the case.

The left-hand side menu is for navigation. It contains buttons to (1) go back to Home view, (2) start the tutorial, (3) Learn about the authors, (4) Select and track the chapters. Each chapter contains one or more case studies. These will be displayed in a sub-menu once the chapter is selected. The cases with a red border allow for interactive exploration of the data. It is also possible to click on a country to explore only the cases present in that country.
